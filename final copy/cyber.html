What is CybePratriot?

Cyber patriot is a national youth cyber defence competition where schools from k-12 compete. This article will give you a basic overview of CyberPatriot.

How it works

There are 3 operating systems (os) in the competition. There is a maximum of 5 members per team. Your goal is to find all vulnerabilities in the os. The os are Linux, Windows server, and Windows, there is also Cisco, but the objective on that is different. Each os has vulnerabilities placed inside. Once the vulnerability has been dealt with you get awarded points. Points are out of 100 on each os. There are multiple different rounds. Each round is 6 hours long, as the rounds go on the vulnerabilities increase and become harder to find. In addition to the os, there is Cisco. Cisco is a textbook-type part of the round. Before the round, you must cover up to a certain section within the Cisco book. There will be a test and a packet tracer to go along with it.

Ranks are given after round two.

Ranks

Ranks round 3

  • The top 30% of all teams go to platinum tier.

  • Top 31-70% of all teams go to gold tier.

  • Top 70-100% of all teams got to silver tier.

Ranks round 4

  • Top 25% of platinum move on to platinum semi-finals

  • Top 25% of gold move on to platinum semi-finals

  • Top 25% of silver move on to platinum semi-finals

Ranks round 5

Top 12 teams in platinum move on to the national finals competition.
