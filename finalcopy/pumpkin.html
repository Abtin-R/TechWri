How to Make a Jack-O-Lantern

Have you ever wanted to carve a basic jack-o-lantern? Do you have no idea how to even start carving a jack-o-lantern? Then you have come to the right place. You will learn how to make a pumpkin similar to this one:

Instructions

Step 1: Go to a store that sells pumpkins and look for your desired size. Make sure that the pumpkin has a flat bottom, sturdy stem, and is not bruised.

Step 2: Upon getting the pumpkin you will need to look for a carving tool kit that looks something like this:

Step 3: Use the carving tool to cut a circular hole in your pumpkin from the stem that looks similar to this:

“Carving tool and Cut out hole”

Step 4: Grab the cutout piece by the stem, and pull it out.

Step 5: Use the scooping tool to clean out everything inside the pumpkin. Once empty, it should look like this:

Step 6: To start making the face, get a type of marker and draw two eyes, a nose, and a mouth that looks like this:

Step 7: Use the carving tool to cut the shapes you just drew out.

Step 8: Put a candle at the bottom of the pumpkin and put the top piece back on.

Now you have made your first basic jack-o-lantern. Remember, you can always improve the design by drawing different shapes.
